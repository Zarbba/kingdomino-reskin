Space Domino

Game phases:

On each turn a player goes completes two phases; placement and selection. (On the first turn there is no placement phase)

Placement phase:

Choose a location to place your tile by clicking on a square in your board. Tiles must be placed so at least one half is touching either your home square or another square of a matching colour. You can rotate tiles by pressing the "Rotate Tile" button. If you are unable to place a tile for any reason, you can discard it by pressing the "Discard Tile" button.

Selection phase:

Claim a tile from the available tile display by clicking on the tile you wish to claim. Tiles are organised from least to most valuable with the least valuable being at the top. You can not claim a tile that has been claimed by another player.

Turn order:

On the first turn, a random player is chosen as the first player and each player proceeds in a clockwise direction from there. On subsequent turns, the player who claimed the top tile in the "Claimed Tiles" display acts first, followed by the next tile in the display until all players have acted.

Scoring:

When there are no more tiles to place the game ends and the scores are computed. Scoring works as follows: Count each section of othoganlly contiguous matching colours. Multiply the number of these squares by the additive total of multipliers in the same zone. Add all your points from each zone together to get your final total. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins!
